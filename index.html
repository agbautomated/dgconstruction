Who We Are

A standard of excellence since 1982

DG Construction is a Bahamian owned company that has held a standard for providing quality and excellent service for more than four decades. Throughout those years, the company has been awarded the opportunity to successfully become part of many major projects here in The Bahamas.

Today the company remains actively involved in projects across the country, with deep experience spanning six project disciplines and a dedicated Specialty Roofing System Division.

Specialty Roofing System Division

The roof is where our standard is tested.

In The Bahamas, the roof takes the sun every day, the rain every season, and the wind when it matters most. That is why DG Construction operates a dedicated Specialty Roofing System Division rather than treating roofing as one trade among many.

Roofing

New Roofing Systems
